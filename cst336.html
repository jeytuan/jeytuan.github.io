Overview of CST 336 - Internet Programming.

  • October 12, 2021
  • By Justin Nguyen

Course Description ..

Internet Programming is an engaging course that delves into the dynamic world of web development and programming. Through a blend of theoretical concepts, hands-on coding projects, and real-world applications, students will gain a comprehensive understanding of the technologies and techniques used in building interactive and robust web applications.

Internet programming: where HTML, CSS, and JavaScript team up to bring your ideas to life and occasionally drive you crazy with their quirks.

In this course, students will explore client-side and server-side web programming languages, such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, NodeJS, and databases. They will learn how to design and develop user-friendly web interfaces, implement responsive layouts, handle form submissions, and create dynamic web pages that interact with databases. Students will also dive into important web development concepts, including security, performance optimization, and scalability, to ensure the creation of efficient and reliable web applications.
