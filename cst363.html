Overview of CST 363 - Database Systems.

  • January 9, 2022
  • By Justin Nguyen

Course Description ..

Database Systems provides a comprehensive exploration of the principles, concepts, and techniques involved in designing, implementing, and managing modern database systems. Students will gain a solid understanding of database design, data modeling, query languages, and database management systems. Through hands-on projects, they will work with popular database systems like MySQL, PostgreSQL, or Oracle, learning to create tables, define relationships, and execute complex queries. By the end of the course, students will possess the skills necessary to design, build, and manage robust database systems effectively.

Database systems: where data dances to the beat of queries and never forgets the steps.

This course students will engage in practical exercises to reinforce their knowledge of database systems. They will learn to apply their understanding of data organization and retrieval, as well as optimization techniques, to ensure efficient and reliable database performance. By working with real-world scenarios and industry-standard tools, students will develop the necessary expertise to solve data-related challenges and contribute to the successful implementation of database systems in various professional settings..
