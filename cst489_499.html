Overview of CST 489/499 - Capstone Project.

  • October 12, 2021
  • By Justin Nguyen

Course Description ..

Capstone Project provides an immersive and culminating experience for students to apply their knowledge and skills in a real-world project. In the AI Capwn project, students will focus on the intersection of artificial intelligence (AI) and decentralized finance (DeFI) to develop a bug bounty sandbox. Through collaborative teamwork and hands-on implementation, students will tackle complex challenges in AI-driven security testing and contribute to the advancement of secure and robust DeFI systems.

In the world of AI Capwn, we chase bugs, hack code, and tame the wild frontiers of decentralized finance, making the digital realm a safer playground for all.

In this capstone project, students will design and develop an AI-powered bug bounty sandbox that utilizes machine learning techniques to identify vulnerabilities and potential exploits within DeFI protocols. They will integrate cutting-edge AI algorithms with smart contract auditing tools to provide a comprehensive security assessment platform. By working on this project, students will gain practical experience in AI, blockchain, and cybersecurity, while exploring the exciting field of DeFI and contributing to the improvement of decentralized financial systems.
